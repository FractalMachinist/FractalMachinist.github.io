Flyers of Usalama

As the MSS Magpie glides into the bustling port of Usalama, a raucous flock of trained crows descends upon the ship. Each bird carries a bundle of colorful flyers, newsletters, and advertisements—courtesy of the Early Bird Flyer Co.—and demands a snack in exchange for its delivery. The party is soon awash in a flurry of local news, shop ads, and event notices, offering a glimpse into the vibrant life of the city.

You’ll find a selection of these flyers attached. They’re meant to give you a sense of what’s available in Usalama and to help you decide what your characters might want to buy or explore. Please note: I will not be roleplaying shopping or haggling during the session. If you want to purchase equipment, potions, or other goods, please decide what you want and have a list ready before we play. You’re welcome to roleplay visiting shops, asking questions, or exploring the city, but shopping transactions themselves will be handled outside of the session.

You’re not expected or required to read every flyer—just skim what interests you. After covering your basic living and professional expenses, you have about 2,000gp left to spend on specific supplies and personal goods.

Let me know if you have any questions or need help with your shopping list!
