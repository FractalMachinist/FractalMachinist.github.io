Council Watch Uncovers Plot Against Academy
In a stunning turn of events, the Royal Council’s automata intercepted a coded message from Lord Halvyr, revealing a plot against the prestigious Royal Mirabilis Academy. Sources within the Council Watch report that the message detailed plans to disrupt the Academy’s upcoming convocation, with the aim of undermining the city’s magical leadership. Swift action by the Council’s agents led to the apprehension of several conspirators, though Lord Halvyr himself remains at large.
“All correspondence is subject to review by the Crown’s Watchful Eye.”
Officials urge vigilance and encourage anyone with information regarding Lord Halvyr’s whereabouts to come forward. Security has been increased around the Academy, and additional patrols are monitoring key locations throughout Usalama.
Let this serve as a warning to those who would conspire in secret—no message is truly private. The safety of Usalama depends on the watchfulness of its people and the diligence of its guardians.
By Gazette Staff
